## Word of Radiance

Classes: **Cleric**  
Level: **0**  

School: **Evocation**  
Element: **Light**  

Casting Time: **1 action**  
Duration: **Instantaneous**  
Range: **Self (5-foot radius)**  
Components: **V, F**
